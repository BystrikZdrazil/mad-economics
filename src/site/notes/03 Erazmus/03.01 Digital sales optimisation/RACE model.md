- A framework to improve the value from digital marketing
- Race consist of 4 steps to help engage customers with brands throughout the costumer lifecycle
- Remember – digital channels always work best when integrated with offline channels

# Reach
Reach involves **building awareness** of a brand and products on other websites and in offline media in order to build traffic by driving visits to your main site or social media pages. It is maximizing reach to create multiple interactions using different [[03 Erazmus/03.01 Digital sales optimisation/Digital media#3 key online media types\|Paid, owned, earned media]] touchpoints.

# Act
It's about persuading site visitors to **take the next step** on their journey when they reach your site. This means (*esp. B2B*) **generating leads** or finding out more about a company.  It's about engaging the audience through relevant, compelling **content** and **clear navigation** so that they don't exit your site. The bounce rates on many sites is more than 50%, so getting the audience to act is a major challenge which is why it is seen as a  separate process.

# Convert
This is **conversion to sale**. It involves getting your audience to take that vital next step which turns them into paying customers whether the payment is taken through online Ecommerce transactions or offline channels.

# Engage
This is developing a **long-term relationship** with first-time buyers to build customer loyalty as repeat purchases using communications on your site, social presence, email and direct interactions to **boost customer lifetime value**. It can be measured by repeat actions such as **repeat sale and sharing content** through social media. We also need to measure the percentage of active customers (*or email subscribers*) and customer satisfaction and recommendation using other systems.

# Smart insights for digital marketing

| **RACE element** | **Aim**                                                                                                                                          | **Sample KPIs**                                                                                                                        |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| *Reach*        | Build awareness across paid and earned media to drive awareness, preference and visits to owned media such as websites and company social pages. | - Unique visitors and fans<br>- Audience share<br>- Revenue or goal value per visit<br>- Brand search volume and share of conversation |
| *Act*          | Encourage audience to interact with brand on desktop and mobile and other online presences to generate leads.                                    | - Bounce rate<br>- Pages per visit<br>- Conversion to lead                                                                             |
| *Convert*      | Achieve conversion to sales and maximise average order value.                                                                                    | - Conversion rate to sale<br>- Average order value<br>- Revenue and margin                                                             |
| *Engage*       | Build customer and fan relationships through time to achieve retention goals and encourage sharing.                                              | - % active customers<br>- Customer satisfaction ratings<br>- Customer advocacy<br>- Repeat conversion rate                             |

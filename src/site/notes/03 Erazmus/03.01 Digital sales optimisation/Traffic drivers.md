# 7 types of traffic drivers
- **Direct** – users type your URL
- **Organic** – search engine traffic
- **Referral** – links from other sites
- **Paid** – PPC, display ads
- **E-mail** – links in your emails
- **Social** – social media platforms
- **Other** – unknown/mixed sources

# Why traffic matters
- The website should be **easily findable**
- Traffic is foundation of all online sales
- **Web traffic** - number of visitors + page views
- Not all traffic is equal
- Never rely on single traffic source

# The importance of mobile
- **87%** of users use a **smartphone** worldwide
- In Denmark **more than half of online purchases** were made using a **smartphone** in 2025
- Mobile changes how and where customer interact with brands

## Things to make sure
- Mobile landing pages (*mobile first*)
- Mobile video (*right sizing*)
- Mobile content consumption (*no audio = subs*)

## Affiliate marketing
- Similar to influencer marketing and often used together
- **Affiliates**: uses personalizes links or discount codes that directly track the effect of the partnership
- **Payment**: Purchase with links/discount codes

| **Pros**                                                                                                                                    | **Cons**                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fast outreach, as it is a fast way to gain new leads and customers, especially when you’re not the one dong the promoting your affiliate is | Potential use of unethical tactics, as profits are more important to them sometimes |
| High traffic                                                                                                                                | Low level of control of how the affiliate reaches the audience                      |
| Affordable cost compared to other means                                                                                                     |                                                                                     |

## Influencer marketing
- Word of mouth
- 37% higher purchase rate than order types of customers
- Cheaper than PPC: advertise directly to your target audience
- Influencers know their audience
- Micro influencers: **10k - 50k** followers

## Banner ads
- Placing ads on websites (*AdSense*)
- Can be static and animated
- Low click rate
- Can pose as a virus to some

## Content marketing
- Valuable content about how your product/service/business solves a problem
- Through blogposts, online articles, product videos, podcasts,…
- Cost effective
	- **3 times as many leads** as traditional marketing
	- **62% cheaper** than traditional marketing

## Co-registration
When user **accepts an offer** from other participants, they are given the **option to subscribe** to **other** information as well.
- E.G. contests on social media or BabyBox
- May or may not have an effect on what you are looking for

## E-mail marketing & newsletters
- ROI is extremely high (*4300% in the USA*)
- **56% are more likely** to buy with a **personalised** experience
- Personalised message in the email -> **18.8% opening rate** VS **13.1% without**
- **Content**: 80% educational + 20% promotional
- **Goal**: drive traffic to sales and connect with customers

## Pay per click (PPC)
Advertisers bid on **keywords so their ads appear in search results** (*Google Ads and Bing Ads*)

PPC **success depends** on:
- Relevant keywords
- High‑quality, optimized landing pages with clear CTAs
- Strong Quality Score (higher relevance = lower cost)

Despite higher costs, PPC **can be effective**:
- High purchase intent
- Measurable ROI
- Increased brand awareness

## Lead magnets
- **Free**, **high value content offer** (*e.g. checklist, template, video*) in exchange for their contact information
- The more relevant and useful the offer, the higher the trust and conversion.

**Effective lead magnets** include:
- Cheat sheets
- Templates
- Free training (videos/workbooks)
- Swipe files
- Toolkits

## SEO
- Organic (*unpaid*) traffic from search engines like Google by improving a website’s visibility in search results

### Why SEO matters
- Most purchase journeys **start with a search** (*90%*)
- Users **mainly click organic** results (*70%*)
- Higher rankings drive more traffic and conversions
- **75%** never make it past the first page on google

### SEO consists of
- **On-page SEO**: keywords, titles, headings, links and content
- **Off-page SEO**: backlinks, social signals and reviews

## Social media
- Strongly **influences buying decisions** and plays a key role in digital marketing
- Builds trust and credibility
- Supports SEO and website visibility
- Increases conversion rates and customer value

## Podcasts
- Another type of **free high value content** about a topic related to your product/business/services
- Suitable for **service/coaching** and **consulting** business

| **Podcasts**              | **Webinars**                                          |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Not live                  | Live/engagement with audience                         |
| Cheap and easy to produce | More common since Covid                               |
| Extension of blogging     | Can drive traffic and sales to your websites/services |

## YouTube videos & In-Stream ads
- Videos are more likely to drive sales in general
- YouTube is one of the most powerful platforms for reach and engagements
- Can increase purchase intent, brand awareness and traffic

### In-Stream ads
- Before or during videos
- Are skippable (*pay only if viewed*)
- Must grab attention in the first 5 seconds
- Focus on the viewer's problem and a clear CTA

# AIDA stages
- **Awareness**
	- Articles
	- Ads
	- Webinars or podcasts
	- Paid search / landing pages
- **Interest**
	- Web content
	- Social media and blogs
	- Newsletters
	- E-mail campaigns
- **Desire**
	- Whitepapers
	- E-books
	- Brochures
	- Microsites
- **Action**
	- Case studies
	- Testimonials
	- Data sheets
	- E-learning

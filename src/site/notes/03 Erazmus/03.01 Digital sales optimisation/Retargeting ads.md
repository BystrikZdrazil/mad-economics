It is showing paid ads to users who already interacted with your business
- Remarketing/retargeting:  “Second chance marketing”
- Visited site but didn’t buy
- Clicked email but didn’t convert

**Why is this effective?**
- Users already know you
- Higher conversion rates
- Lower cost than cold ads
- Similar logic to email

# Website retargeting (*pixel-based*)
**What it is**
- Tracks users who visit your website using a tracking pixel (*cookie*)

**How it works**
- User visits your website
- A tracking pixel records the visit
- Ads are shown later (*e.g. on Google, Facebook*)

>[!example]+
>You visit Zalando → later see shoe ads on Instagram
>
>**Purpose**: Bring visitors back to complete a purchase

# E-mail retagreting
Targets users based on their email behavior

**How it works**
- Doesn’t open emails
- Clicks but doesn’t buy
- Abandons a cart

>[!example]+
>“*You forgot something in your cart*”
>
>“*Still interested in this product?*”
>
>**Purpose**: Re-engage users already in your email list

# Social media retargeting
Target users on social platforms using data from:
- Website visits
- Email lists
- App activity

**How it works**
- Upload email list OR use pixel data
- Platforms (*Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn*) match users
- Show targeted ads

>[!example]+
>You sign up for a newsletter: later see brand ads on Facebook
>
>**Purpose**: Stay visible across platforms (*omni-channel*)

# Retargeting compared
| **Type**    | **Data Source**        | **Example**             | **Strength**      |
| ----------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------- | ----------------- |
| *Website*     | Site visits            | “Come back and buy” ads | Broad reach       |
| *Email*       | Email behavior         | Cart reminders          | Direct + personal |
| *Social*      | Platforms (Meta, etc.) | Ads in feed             | Visibility        |
| *Dynamic ads* | Product views          | Same product in ad      | High conversion   |

# Google ads retargeting
**Step 1**
- You set it all up in Google Ads

**Step 2**
- Go to Audience Manager → Audience Sources → Google Ads tag
- Choose Remarketing from website
- Install the tag on your website

**Step 3**
- Set up a new campaign
- When selecting a target audience, choose Website Visitors

**Step 4**
- Select what kind of ad you want
- Click “*Create Campaign*”

# Facebook retargeting
**Step 1**
- Go to Business Manager
- Find the Facebook Pixel
- Add it to your website

**Step 2**
- Set up your customer audience (*inside Business Manager*)
- Select website visitors

**Step 3**
- Create the actual Facebook Ad campaign
- **Audience**: people who have visited your website

# Content optimisation
**Good SEO content** is:
- Helpful and user-focused
- Structured with headings
- Written in natural language (*not keyword stuffing*)
- Updated and relevant

**Google & AI tools prefer**
- Clear answers
- Authority
- Real value

# Internal linking
- Connect pages on your website
- Help search engines understand structure
- Increase time on site

Best practise
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Link from strong pages to important sales pages

# User experience in SEO
- Great design and usability = good SEO
	- Longer sessions on the site
	- Page 1
	- Top 3 search positions on Google

**Pains**:
- Slow sites
- Bad design
- Not having mobile responsive

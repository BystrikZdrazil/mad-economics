# Why it matters
- More than 90% of purchases online start with a google search
- Organic traffic = more trust than ads
- SEO reduces dependency on paid media
- Strong SEO = lower cost per acquisition (*CPA*)

# How search engine works
- **Crawling** - bots scan websites
- **Indexing** - content is stored and categorized
- **Ranking** - results are ranked on quality

## Crawling
You can think of crawling as Google discovering you page. The bots scan your site and "*read*" it like a browser does.

. . .

## Indexing
After a page is crawled, Google tries to understand what it is about and categorize it.
- Text, headings, images, and structure are analyzed
- The page is stored in Google's massive database (*the index*)
- . . .

## Ranking
When a user searches for something.

Google ranks pages based on signals like:
- Relevance to the query
- Content quality
- Authority and trust
- User experience

What's important SEO-wise
- Better matching intent = higher ranking
- Helpful content beats keyword stuffing
- Quality > quantity

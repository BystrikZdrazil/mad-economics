# Value proposition
- To create a better everyday life for the many people
- Be the most affordable furnisher

# Main digital channels
- The IKEA website
- IKEA app
- Social media
- Ads (*YouTube, TV*)

# Customer data and company needs
- Collecting data through the IKEA family program
- Signing in for delivery or assembly
- Purchase history
- In-store operational data

# Most important KPIs
- Sales volume
- Environmental sustainability
- Price compared to competition

# Biggest strategic challenge
- Remaining as the "*affordability king*" with rising operational costs and lower purchasing power
- Restructuring, workforce reductions to become simpler and faster 

# Room for improvement
- Still be affordable, but focus on the products actually working, not just looking good

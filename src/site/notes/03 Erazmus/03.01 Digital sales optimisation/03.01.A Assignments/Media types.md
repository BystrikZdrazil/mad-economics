# 3 key online media types
- Paid is advertising you pay for (*ads*)
- Owned is content you control (*website, social profiles*)
- Earned is publicity gained through third-party recognition (*reviews, shares, PR*)

# Why is paid media rarely enough on its own
- Just because you pay for advertising doesn't mean people will like it

>[!example]+
>**Paid advertisement** for a powerful laptop that **pushes** the specs onto the customer

# Why is owned media critical for long-term value
- Because people may subconsciously judge things based on the presentation, not necessarily on the quality
- Having a presence that doesn't require constant payments to a 3rd party (*like paid ads*) is crucial for long-term success

>[!example]+
>A good website that makes it **easy to understand** why this laptop is great for specific use-cases. Helps the customer **decide** and sometimes **compare** with older models or competition

# Why is earned media the hardest but most valuable
- It is the most convincing type of media
- The ones that are promoting the product aren't affiliated in any way
- Acting on their own with no prompts or scripts

>[!example]+
>**My friend** tells me about the laptop, about what he liked and dislikes. This **honest review** from **someone I trust** is therefore the most valuable, as he has no affiliation with the company

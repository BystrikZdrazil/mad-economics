# The Key Drivers (What brings people in)
- **Insider Access:** Readers pay for what they can’t find for free—exclusive "behind-the-scenes" scoops and verified gossip that feels like a leaked secret.
- **The "Me-Zine" Effect:** Using AI to customize the feed so a streetwear fan doesn't see nothing but ballgowns. Personalization keeps people from hitting "unsubscribe."
- **Shop-the-Look:** Making the magazine a tool. If a reader can buy the exact shoes in a gossip photo with one click, the magazine becomes a "must-have" shopping assistant.
- **Community Perks:** Moving beyond just reading. Think private chat groups, digital events, or early access to fashion drops.

# The Key Measures (How you know it's working)
- **Churn Rate:** The most vital stat. It tells you exactly how many people are leaving each month. If this is high, your content isn't "sticky" enough.
- **Trial-to-Paid Conversion:** This measures if your "teasers" are strong enough to make people actually pull out their credit cards.
- **Saves & Shares:** In 2026, a "Like" is cheap. A "Save" means your fashion advice is actually useful, and a "Share" means your gossip is trending.
- **Frequency of Use:** How many times a week a subscriber opens the app. For a "HOT" brand, you want them checking daily so they don't miss a beat.

- **Marketing technology** – any tech solution that supports a marketing goal
- All the tools a company uses to learn about, connect with, and deliver experiences to their customers
- MarTech ecosystems or **MarTech stacks** are all the different marketing technology solutions a given company/brand uses to help execute their marketing strategy
- These technologies (*e.g., CRM, analytics, social media, and automation tools*) are interconnected to streamline workflows, enhance customer engagement, and improve ROI
- The MarTech landscape (*obviously*) keeps growing
	- There are **more than 15000 MarTech tools** as of 2026

# Key Components
- **Customer Data & CRM**: Platforms like Salesforce or HubSpot store customer information, track interactions, and manage leads.
- **Content Management System (*CMS*)**: Tools like WordPress or Contentful allow teams to create, publish, and manage digital content.
- **Marketing Automation**: Software like Marketo or ActiveCampaign enables automating repetitive tasks and nurturing leads.
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Tools such as Google Analytics and Mixpanel provide performance insights to optimize strategies.
- **Advertising Technology (*Adtech*)**: Platforms like StackAdapt help manage ad campaigns and media buying
- **Social Media & Content Distribution**: Tools like Hootsuite are used for scheduling and monitoring social media interaction.

# Key Benefits of a Strong Martech Stack
- **Increased Productivity**: Automates manual tasks, saving time and resources.
- **Improved Insights**: Provides a 360-degree view of the customer, facilitating better data-driven decision-making.
- **Enhanced Personalization**: Enables tailored messaging based on customer behavior and preferences.
- **Better Collaboration**: Connects different teams and workflows

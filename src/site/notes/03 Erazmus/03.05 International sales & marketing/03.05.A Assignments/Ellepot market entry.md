**Statistics:**
- 6 continents
- 20 partners
- 130 countries
- 10 billion ellepots a year

# Expansion to Slovakia

## Transaction specific factors
- Tacit nature of know-how
- Opportunistic behaviour

## Internal and external factors
- **Internal factors**
	- Firm size is quite small
	- International experience is very high, as they are in more than 130 countries
- **External factors**
	- Sociocultural distance between home country and host country is not that different, as both of the countries are in the EU
	- Slovakia is a medium risk country, as it has a fair amount of farms and does a lot of growing plants, but the people aren’t always sustainability-focused
	- Market size and growth - market size is fair, but there is not much growth potential
	- [[03 Erazmus/03.05 International sales & marketing/Market entry strategy#External Factors\|Direct and indirect trade barriers]]
	- There is little to none competition in Slovakia

## Control VS risk
The company would probably prefer control, as they have 20 partners but are in 130 countries.

## Opportunism and intermediary problems
Slovak people are quite opportunistic and would try to milk the business as much as possible. There may therefore be some problems regarding the costs of selling in Slovakia.

# 7 attitudes in change management
1. Denial
2. Resistance
3. Acceptance
4. Exploration
5. Commitment
6. A proactive desire to disrupt the status quo
7. Possession of a vision for transformational change

# Reasons for change
- Technological development
	- Shortens the PLC
- Political events
	- Increased focus on sustainability
- Increased globalisation
	- Changes competitive conditions
- Companies’ growing size, complexity and specialisation
	- Requires greater knowledge from managers to employees

# 3 overall types
1) Qualitative (judgement/counting) techniques
No or limited amount of data
Based on human judgement

2) Time series analysis
Many years of data available to identify:
- Seasonal fluctuations
- Cyclical fluctuations (2-3 years)
- Trends
- Growth/decline rates


<!-- Slide number: 6 -->
# 3 overall types

3) Causal models

Includes historical data and finds relationships between internal data and external influences such as:
Political, economic, social and technological developments, as well as related/competing companies

# Bias in forecasting
Bias in forecasting
Bias/misunderstandings:
Wishful thinking.. Looking for signs supporting what you already believe will happen
Pressure to deliver convincing forecasts rather than realistic scenarios
Tendency >< certainty

# Purpose
- Identifies individuals, groups, or organizations that influence or are affected by the project
- Helps prioritize stakeholders based on importance, power, and interest
- Ensures communication and engagement strategies are aligned with stakeholder needs
- Modern project ecosystems include freelancers, contractors, AI agents, strategic partners, and customers

# Stakeholder Matrix (Power–Interest Grid)
- Categorizes stakeholders by:
    - High power / high interest
    - High power / low interest
    - Low power / high interest
    - Low power / low interest
- Supports targeted communication and involvement strategies

>[!example]+
>- Used in class challenge for “Greenland – Denmark – USA project”
>- Students identify stakeholders and place them according to importance
>- Examples shown from Taleia and student newspapers

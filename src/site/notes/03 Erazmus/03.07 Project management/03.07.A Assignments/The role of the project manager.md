# What does a project manager do?
- Manages the people of a team
- Schedules the timeline
- Talks to the board/superiors/contractors
- Gathers knowledge
- Makes key decisions

# Who is a Project Manager?
- Project Manager - a person in charge of a project - should (be):
    - Helps lead the project towards success while aligning different parts and aspects of the project - controlling and organizing - **budget management**
    - Coordinates a team of different specialities in order to achieve a goal = ensure **teamwork**
    - Sets responsibilities for different members of the team = **milestones and goals**
    - Makes sure that deadlines are met = **time-management and supervising**
        - Multitasking + predicting and setting initial timelines
    - Motivates
    - Negotiates deals
    - Manages people
        - Informing important stakeholders and ensuring good knowledge of problems = **well-rounded**
    - Strong and independent leader + flexible = being able to **adapt**
        - Information gathering + decision making
    - Structured and challenging work
    - Overview over things - SWOT analysis
        - Risk management
    - Delegates tasks - who? when? what?
    - communicative and sympathetic personality + structured and assertive
        - Handling conflicts
    - Emotionally intelligent + analytical
    - Settle & ensure good and comfortable work culture

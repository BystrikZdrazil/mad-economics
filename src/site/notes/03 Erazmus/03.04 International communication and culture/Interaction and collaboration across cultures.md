# Corruption
Corruption in business involves **abusing** entrusted **power** for **private gain**, often through **bribery**, **fraud**, or **extortion**.

Corruption damage reputation and causing severe **financial** and **legal risks**.

**Common forms** include **bribery** in public procurement, **embezzlement**, and **collusion with officials**. It **undermines competition** inflate costs, and reduces productivity.

Each **year**, in both **developed** and **developing** countries, over **1 trillion USD** is paid in **bribes**.

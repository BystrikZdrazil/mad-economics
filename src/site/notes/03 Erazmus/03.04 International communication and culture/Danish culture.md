# Described as
- Extremely egalitarian
- Shy of conflicts (*focusing on consensus*)
- Direct/non-formal
- Private and reserved
- Punctual
- Individualistic
- Huge mutual trust
- Independent
- Nonreligious/skeptical

# Communication
- Ironical and using sarcasm
- Include humour as a way to break the ice
- Implied meanings
- Informal/direct and with great honesty
- Moderate feelings
- Focus on consensus
- Do not respond positive to orders

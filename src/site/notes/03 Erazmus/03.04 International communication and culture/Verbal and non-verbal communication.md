# Non-verbal communications
- Posture
- Appearance
- Head movements
- Hand movements
- Eye movements
- Facial expression
- Body contact
- Closeness
- Sounds
- Ways of talking

# Subconscious norms
- Individualist assertiveness (*boasting*) vs. collectivist modesty (*self-effacing*).
- Latin "2 speakers" (*disinterest*) vs. Japanese "2 listeners" (*disinterest*).
- Latin interruptions (*rude*) vs. Anglo-Saxon "stage" (*boring*).
- Western eye contact (*rude*) vs. Eastern eye lowering (*lack of honesty, inattentive*).

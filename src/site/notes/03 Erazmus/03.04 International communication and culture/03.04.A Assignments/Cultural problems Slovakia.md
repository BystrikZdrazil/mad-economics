# Answer
- Boundaries are not a problem as we have a high power distance score
- Equality is a problem in Slovakia, as people with more power tend to abuse it
- As Slovakia has high masculinity, we definitely work with others
- Uncertainty is not a problem in Slovakia, as there are some companies that take risks and some that don’t, leaving us with a pretty balanced score
- Dealing with time may be a problem in Slovakia, as there is a lot of bureaucracy and some companies aren’t that oriented on the long-term goal

## Slovakia’s biggest problems
- Bureauracy
- Lack of trust among people
- Inequality in the workplace
- High masculinity in socialist government

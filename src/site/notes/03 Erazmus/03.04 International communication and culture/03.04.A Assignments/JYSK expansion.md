| More relatable               | Needs research |
| Great for different cultures |                |

# Plan for JYSK expansion
For countries similar to Denmark
- Keep the same advertising as home

For vastly different countries
1. Create an image of the average customer in said country
2. Develop their needs and wants
3. Study their interactions and buying behaviour (*spontaneous / thinking*)
4. Address their needs
5. Market the products in a way that doesn't offend them and if there are jokes included, make sure they are appropriate

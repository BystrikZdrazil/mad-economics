# Meaning
- **Power distance**
	- Great power distance
	- Very hierarchical
		- Even in schools
	- A lot of bureaucracy
- **Individualism**
	- Slovakia is quite individualistic, but not much, there is still some social pressure to be on the same level
- **Masculinity**
	- Highest possible, people need to be heroes, work hard and provide for their families
- **Uncertainty avoidance**
	- Mid risk taking
	- Slovakia doesn't want to do very risky things, but they will experiment with multiple options for sure
- **Long term orientation**
	- This must be a joke as the parliament doesn't know what they will do this summer, it should be way low
- **Indulgence**
	- People don't enjoy themselves too much
	- IMO because of religion, which is strict and tells us to behave

# Important info for business
- Slovaks aren't that honest
- They will try to capitalise on every opportunity you give them
- Very high ego in patriots, but normal levels against everyone else
- Slovaks don't like to work hard
- Everything takes a lot of time

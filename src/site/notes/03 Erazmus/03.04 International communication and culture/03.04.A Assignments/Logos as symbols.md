# Apple
- High quality
- Great user experience
- Great ecosystem
- Great overall products
- Luxurious
- Lifestyle brand
- Sometimes overpriced
- Attention to details

## Symbolic value
Even though Apple products are expensive, sometimes even overpriced, they are always great quality.

If you use an Apple product, you can see the attention to details that make it almost magical with most of the products.

# Nike
The Nike brand represents great clothing and footwear for all kinds of sportsmen, either professional athletes or just a hobbyist gym member. The clothing is of high quality, engineered for perfection in multiple sports.

The Swoosh logo represents in my opinion the speed with which a runner passes through the finish line.

# Framework
The logo symbolizes the repairability and upgradeability of the hardware, offering complete control to the customer over their device.

- It is an **approach to leading** and **optimizing** a company's **sales team**

- **Increase** sales **efficiency**
- **Enhance** customer **relationships**
- **Boost** sales **revenue**
- **Improve** sales team **performance**

# Incentives
There are 2 variations that, while they aren't this extreme in the real world, set the climate in a company based on set criteria.

## Formal realistic evaluation
- Must deserve salary
- Sales figures - average
- Target negotiated
- Rewards and sanctions **based on results**
- **Individualism**
- Low PD, UA, explicit

## Internal incentive
- Cannot be dismissed
- **Close personal ties**
- **Boss knows best** - motivate not achieve
- **Collectivism**
- High PD, UA, implicit

# Underperformance

## Masculine
- Clear warning
- Dismissal

## Feminine
- More understanding
- Explanation
- Assessment
- Assistance
- Final decision

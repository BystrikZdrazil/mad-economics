# Strategy
## Innovation
We believe that technology can and should work for the good and that the meaningful innovation can and will contribute to a better world in ways big and small.

## Diversity and inclusion
We thrive on diverse voices. We engage the experiences, strengths, and diverse perspectives of our employees and customers to inform, challenge, and stretch our thinking. That’s how we create innovation.

## Corporate social responsibility
We believe that technology can strengthen the cause of good, and work to promote a sustainable future where everyone has access to the benefits and opportunities that technology creates

## AI
We believe that when AI is developed with humans in mind, it can expand your capabilities, free up your energy for more creative and strategic endeavours, and help you or your organisation achieve more.

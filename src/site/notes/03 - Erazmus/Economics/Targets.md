What is measured usually is also achieved easier. Targets establish the level of performance or rate of improvement required for a measure (KPI)
- Typically **set by management**
- Should be set to **represent excellent performance**
- Should, if achieved, place the company as one of the **best performers in its industry**
- Should create **distinctive value for customers**

Targets are important, as without a clear goal it can not be achieved.

# Targets and timeframes
- **Long term targets**
	- Monumental challenges
	- Takes years to conquer (10-30)
	- Cannot be achieved through business as usual
- **Midrange targets**
	- Breaking the long term targets into smaller segments
	- 3-5 years
	- Realistic but still motivating
- **Short term targets**
	- *“A journey of 1000 miles begins with a single step”*
	- Annual basis (or even shorter)
	- Early warning system, timely feedback

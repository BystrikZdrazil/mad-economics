# Strategy

## Quantitative

### Survey
- Asking a larger amount of people a set of predefined questions
- Marking the answers and explaining them in numbers

### Experiment
- Performing certain actions
- Observations

## Qualitative

### Archival research
- What happened in the past

### Case study
- Using a certain subject as an object of study
- *Why is this like this?*
- *How does this work?*

### Etnography
- Studying the psychology of people
- Behavioural analysis

### Action research
- Ourselves included in the research
- Informed or uninformed people (about the study)

### Grounded theory
- Building up the theory
- Created knowledge in the process of doing research

# Time horizon

## Cross sectional

## Longitudinal
- Longer researches (*1+ years*)
- Ongoing observation / conducting research

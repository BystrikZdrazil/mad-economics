Gartner is an organisation for business leaders that covers SCM

# Top 25 methodology
- To be considered for Gartner’s Top 25
	- Companies that appear on the most recent Fortune Global 500 or Forbes 2000 lists
	- Companies that generate at least 50% of their revenue from physical products
	- A minimum annual revenue of USD 15 billion
	- A minimum ESG score requirement (S&P Global ESG score) in order to be considered

| **Category**        | **Criteria**                     | **Weighting** |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------- |
| *Business metrics*  | Return on physical assets (ROPA) | 5%            |
| *Business metrics*  | Changes in ROPA                  | 10%           |
| *Business metrics*  | Revenue growth                   | 10%           |
| *Business metrics*  | Inventory turns                  | 5%            |
| *Business metrics*  | ESG                              | 20%           |
| *Community opinion* | Peer vote                        | 25%           |
| *Community opinion* | Gardner expert vote              | 25%           |

![[Pasted image 20260130110044.png\|Pasted image 20260130110044.png]]

The ranking is based on pints awarded for both “business metrics” and “community opinion”

ESG points are awarded for the following:
- **Commitment**
	- If the company follows UN Global Compact
- **Transparency**
	- If the company’s sustainability reports follow the Global Reporting Initiative standards
- **Performance**
	- Divided into 3 categories
		- Environmental
		- Social
		- Governance

# Company 1 (innovative, high-tech manufacturing)
- **Improvement in performance**
	- % improvement between last model and new model
- **Quality assurance** and sustainability
	- Being able to trace the origins of all materials and specific parts
- **Planning development** with the customer
	- Number of customers that are actively helping the company improve

# Company 2 (cost-focused manufacturer)
- Integrated **production planning**
	- Calculating the efficiency of production by comparing the company with other in the same field
- **Waste reduction**
	- Less waste = less sunken costs
	- Can be expressed in the percentage of materials that are thrown away as waste
- **Economies of scale**
	- Using economies of scale to make more products for a lower price per unit
	- Can be measured in the costs per unit
